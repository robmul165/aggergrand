About Us

We are a small team of passionate and experienced growers. We are utilizing our skills in technology to bring digital tools to growers, empowering them to grow healthier and more nutrient dense plants.

We offer personalized recommendations to help optimize plant growth, improve nutrient density, and support better soil management.

By combining hands-on soil testing with data-driven insights, Agger Grand empowers growers to make informed decisions that enhance both their crops and the environment. Whether you’re tending a backyard garden or managing a farm, we’re here to help you build healthier soil for a more abundant future.

Our Work

    👩‍🌾Gardeners Dozens of gardeners have purchased our soils tests and used our recommendations to grow better plants more sustainably. By giving one on one consultations while going above and beyond our research on what you should do, we strive to give accurate answers to your growing area needs.

    🌱 St. Rose of Lima As a private school in New Jersey, they were concerned about having lead in their common grounds. We tested the soil and found high levels of arsenic. We provided a necessary remediation plan.

    🌾 Rancocas Creek Farm The leading certified organic farm in central jersey, we worked with them to study a farmers daily routines as well as give classes and demonstrations about soil health.

    🏛 Rutgers Environmental Stewards Program: We are actively engaged with Rutgers' program, furthering our knowledge and contributing to community soil management initiatives.
