Our Mission

Our mission is to become your gardening calculator.

We are developing a soil database to provide real-time insights into nutrient composition, soil quality, and sustainable growing practices. Our free Soil Calculator offers personalized recommendations to help optimize plant growth, improve nutrient density, and support better soil management.

By combining hands-on soil testing with data-driven insights, Agger Grand empowers growers to make informed decisions that enhance both their crops and the environment. Whether you’re tending a backyard garden or managing a farm, we’re here to help you build healthier soil for a more abundant future.
